ABOUT

CloudClear AI

CloudClear AI is an ISRO Hackathon project that removes clouds from satellite imagery using Artificial Intelligence, cloud segmentation, multi-temporal data fusion and generative reconstruction models. The platform helps improve Earth observation for agriculture, disaster management, urban planning and environmental monitoring.
